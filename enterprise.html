Enterprise

TNM offers first class service to its Enterprise and Individual customers at all times. We strive to always transact business with all our customers in a professional, transparent and honest manner. Our Enterprise and Individual customers are our partners, and we treat them as such.

We have an extensive, high quality and most reliable network coverage throughout Malawi and more than 97% of our Sites are on 3G. We also have the highest number of 4G sites across Malawi.

At the pinnacle of mobile and Enterprise services in Malawi, we understand the wants and needs of businesses operating in Malawi. TNM has on offer a bouquet of products and services for corporate customers in such categories as; Enterprise Office Communications, Enterprise Internet and VPN, Broadband and LTE connectivity solution, Home Internet and Business process outsourcing solutions such as Call Centre as a service.
